Chicken Spaghetti

Description

A satisfying yet simple meal that's guaranteed to fill your tummy and your heart!

Ingredients

  • Angel Hair Pasta
  • 2 cups of cooked chicken breasts
  • Canned condensed cream of chicken soup
  • Canned diced tomatoes
  • Cubed processed cheese
  • Chopped jarred mushrooms
  • Seasonings (salt, pepper, crushed red pepper)

Steps

  1. Cook the pasta, drain it, then return it to the pot.
  2. Add the remaining ingredients to the pot.
  3. Cook, stirring frequently, until the cheese is melted.
